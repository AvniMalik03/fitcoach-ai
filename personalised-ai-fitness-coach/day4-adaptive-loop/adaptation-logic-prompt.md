## Core Challenge
Distinguish "bad day" (isolated, low-signal) vs. "genuine plateau" (repeated, high-signal across weeks) and adjust plans accordingly.

## Bad Day Signals (single occurrence, do not overreact)
- One missed session
- One night of poor sleep
- One low-energy day with otherwise consistent adherence

## Plateau Signals (pattern across 2+ weeks)
- No strength/weight progression across 3+ sessions of the same lift
- Consistent adherence but no biometric movement toward goal
- Repeated high RPE at same load (regression in capacity)
