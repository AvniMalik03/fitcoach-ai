# Day 1 — Onboarding Form Spec (for Figma)

Status: To do

## Purpose
Collect the data needed to generate a personalised, safe, adaptive plan.

## Fields to Collect
1. Goals (weight loss / muscle gain / endurance / general fitness / rehab)
2. Current fitness level (beginner / intermediate / advanced)
3. Dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, allergies, religious/cultural restrictions)
4. Injury history (past & current, affected body part, severity)
5. Available equipment (home / gym / bodyweight only / specific equipment list)
6. Schedule constraints (days/week available, session length, preferred time)
7. Biometrics (age, height, weight, activity level)
